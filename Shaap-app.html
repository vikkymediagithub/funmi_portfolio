Problem Statement

Accessing fast and convenient delivery services on demand can be very challenging due to the lack of a platform that provides them with a variety of options. As a result, this has led to disappointment, poor delivery services, and negative impacts on small businesses that require such services.

Solution

Design a mobile application that allows businesses and individuals to quickly search and hire delivery services near them on demand. This mobile app will allow give user control over their choice such that the can schedule both pickup and delivery time.

What motivate this project?

As a UX designer, I also run a small business on the side, which requires on-demand delivery of my goods. However, the delivery process is often fraught with disappointment and obstacles. I believe this is a common problem faced by many small business owners. This realization has inspired me to create a new product called SHAAP. It's a mobile app designed to help individuals and small business owners bridge the gap with delivery services. With SHAAP, I hope to make a positive impact by improving the delivery experience for everyone involved.
